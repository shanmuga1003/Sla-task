About chennai

Chennai, formerly known as Madras, is the vibrant capital of Tamil Nadu and a major cultural, economic, and educational hub on India's Coromandel Coast. Known as the "Gateway to South India," it is famous for its 13-km Marina Beach (India's longest urban beach), ancient temples like Kapaleeshwarar, colonial history (Fort St. George), and a booming automobile industry, often called the "Detroit of Asia".
