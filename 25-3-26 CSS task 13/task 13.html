IPL 2026

The 2026 Indian Premier League (IPL) is the 19th season of the tournament, featuring 10 teams playing 84 matches across 13 venues from late March 2026. Defending champions Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) open against Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) on March 28, 2026. Cameron Green was the highest-paid player, signed by KKR for ₹25.20 crore (US$2.78 million).

Format: The tournament returns to a double round-robin format, with 10 teams split into two virtual groups, leading to the playoffs and final.

Venues: Matches are scheduled across major cities including Bengaluru, Ahmedabad, Guwahati, Kolkata, Chennai, Lucknow, Delhi, Hyderabad, Mumbai, and Mullanpur.

Auction Highlights: Australian all-rounder Cameron Green was the most expensive player, bought by KKR for $2.78 million (approx. ₹25.2 crore). Indian players Prashant Veer and Kartik Sharma were top buys for Chennai Super Kings.
